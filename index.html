Y Combinator International Founder Award

Recognizing extraordinary entrepreneurial achievement among international founders building the future.

About the Award

In alignment with our mission to advance science and education by supporting entrepreneurship globally, YCombinator.org formally confers the Y Combinator International Founder Award to recognize excellence in entrepreneurship.

This award acknowledges non-U.S. founders whose vision, technical ability, and execution have been validated through Y Combinator's highly competitive selection process—which accepts fewer than 2% of applicants. This rigorous evaluation by experts in entrepreneurship, technology, and venture capital serves as the basis for recognition.

The Award is designed to encourage recipients to pursue high-impact global projects that contribute to scientific and educational progress.

The Y Combinator International Founder Award is conferred by YCombinator.org to non-U.S. founders who have demonstrated exceptional ability through their acceptance into Y Combinator's accelerator program.

Verification

YCombinator.org provides verification of the award upon request to relevant third parties and institutions.

This is a non-monetary award. No cash prize or financial grant is associated with this recognition.

Eligibility

The Y Combinator International Founder Award is available to:

  • Non-U.S. citizens or permanent residents
  • Founders who have been accepted into a Y Combinator batch
  • Individuals whose work demonstrates potential for significant scientific, technological, or educational impact

Selection is determined through the Y Combinator admissions process, which evaluates founders based on their demonstrated ability, the merit of their work, and their potential for exceptional achievement.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is YCombinator.org?

YCombinator.org is a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in 2015 with the mission to advance science and education. YCombinator.org operates as a program of YC ORG.

Is this award associated with Y Combinator the accelerator?

The award is administered by YCombinator.org, a nonprofit entity. Selection is based on acceptance into the Y Combinator accelerator program, which serves as expert evaluation of entrepreneurial excellence.

Is there a monetary prize?

No. This is a non-monetary recognition of achievement. The award consists of formal documentation acknowledging the recipient's accomplishment.

How do I apply?

There is no separate application. Eligible international founders who are accepted into Y Combinator are automatically considered for the award.

Can the award be verified?

Yes. YCombinator.org provides verification of awards upon request to relevant institutions or agencies.
